About Us

Kuat Solutions is an international industrial engineering and project support company operating between Hong Kong and Europe. With our team based in Italy, we work closely with leading European manufacturers, enabling fast negotiations, direct sourcing, and efficient coordination on the ground. Our presence in Hong Kong provides global reach in logistics and trade, while our on-the-ground presence in Italy allows us to support projects with strong local insight and established partnerships. This structure enables us to deliver complex industrial solutions with speed, flexibility, and a high level of execution across regions.
