Bearings

Precision bearings for industrial machinery and equipment

Deep grove ball bearings

Widely used bearings featuring a simple design capable of handling radial and some axial loads at high speeds

Tapered Roller bearings

Designed to handle both radial and axial loads simultaneously, making them ideal for applications like vehicle wheel bearings and gearboxes

Slewing bearings

Large sized bearing, can handle very high loads from several directions

Spherical roller bearings

Bearings capable of handling heavy radial loads due to their barrel shaped bearing elements

Needle roller bearings

Long, slender cylindrical bearing elements enable the handling of high loads within a relatively small radial space

Thrust ball bearings

Designed to support axial loads and are not suitable for handling significant radial loads
